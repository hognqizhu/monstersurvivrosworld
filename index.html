Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this addictive action game!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters and collecting power-ups to enhance your abilities.

With each level, the challenge increases as monsters become stronger and more numerous. Test your skills and see how long you can last in this thrilling battle for survival!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters with unique starting abilities and play styles.

Progressive Difficulty

Face increasingly challenging waves of monsters as you survive longer.

Power-ups & Abilities

Collect various power-ups and unlock new abilities to enhance your survival chances.

Addictive Gameplay

Simple to learn but hard to master, with endless replayability.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement to avoid getting surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize upgrades that complement your character's strengths
  • Collect experience orbs quickly before they disappear
  • Try to maintain a safe distance from large groups of monsters
  • When choosing upgrades, balance offensive and defensive abilities
